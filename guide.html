☕ Coffee Money

A simple guide — how to use your money app

🟢 Start here — set it up once

Do these steps one time. After that, you just tap the ☕ coffee icon on your phone — like any other app.
⚠️ Important: if you got this link in WhatsApp, first open it in your real browser (Chrome or Safari). WhatsApp’s own little window cannot make the icon.
📱 If you have an iPhone
1
Tap the app link to open it.
2
If it opened inside WhatsApp, tap the small compass / Safari icon in the bottom corner to open it in Safari.
3
Tap Share — the little box with an ↑ arrow at the bottom.
4
Scroll down, tap “Add to Home Screen,” then tap Add.
🤖 If you have an Android (Samsung, Huawei…)
1
Tap the app link to open it.
2
If it opened inside WhatsApp, tap ⋮ (three dots, top corner) → “Open in Chrome.”
3
In Chrome, pull the page down once to refresh it.
4
Tap ⋮ (top corner) → “Add to Home screen” (it may say “Install app”) → Add.
✅ Done! A ☕ Coffee Money icon is now on your phone. Just tap it any time — no link needed again.

📌 Already have a plain grey icon from before? Hold it → Remove, then do the steps above again — now it shows the coffee cup. ☕

💡 Don’t want an icon? You can also just tap the link each time — the app works exactly the same.

🔘 The buttons — what each one does

＋ Cash in Money a son (Rashad or Omar) gives you.
－ Cash out Money that goes back out to that son.
🧁 Sales Money the shop makes by selling.
🛒 Buy Money you spend buying things for the shop.

👀 What you see

A box for Rashad and a box for Omar. Each shows:

•
Cash in — money he gave you.
•
Cash out — money that went back out to him.
•
Balance — his money you are still holding (Cash in − Cash out).

Below, The shop shows Sales, Buy, and Profit (Sales − Buy).

✅ A simple example

Rashad gives you 500 → tap ＋ Cash in on Rashad.

Later you give him back 200 → tap － Cash out on Rashad.

The app shows: Rashad Balance = 300. 🎉
And the shop: you sold 1,000,000 and bought 400,000 → Profit = 600,000.

💡 Good to know

•
It works offline — no internet needed after it opens.
•
Your numbers are saved only on your phone — private.
•
Dollars and Lira are separate. Tap LBP or USD at the top to switch between your two books — they never mix.
•
Made a mistake? Tap the small × next to any entry to delete it.

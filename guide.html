A simple guide — how to use your money app

✅ You can use it right now

Just tap the link and start — the app works straight away! 🎉

The icon below is optional — it only saves you from finding the link each time.

📲 To put an icon on your phone (optional)

⚠️ Important: if you opened the link inside WhatsApp, you must first open it in your real browser — WhatsApp's own window can’t make an icon.
📱 On iPhone
1
Tap the small compass / Safari icon in the bottom corner to open it in Safari.
2
Tap Share (the box with an ↑ arrow).
3
Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen.”
🤖 On Android
1
Tap the ⋮ (three dots, top corner) → “Open in Chrome.”
2
In Chrome, tap ⋮ again.
3
Tap “Add to Home screen.”

Now a Coffee Money ☕ icon is on your phone. Tap it like any app — no link needed again.

📌 Already have a plain grey icon from before? Just delete it (hold it → Remove) and add it again — now it shows the coffee cup. ☕

🔘 The buttons — what each one does

＋ Money When Rashad or Omar gives you money.
🛒 Item When you buy something with their money.
↩ Give back When you return some of their money.
🧁 Add a sale When the shop sells something.
💸 Shop expense A shop bill (rent, electricity…).

👀 What you see at the top

Two boxes — one for Rashad, one for Omar. Each shows:

•
Brought in — money they gave you.
•
Items — what you bought with their money.
•
Leftover — their money you have NOT used yet.

Their money never mixes. You always know whose is whose.

✅ A simple example

Rashad gives you 500 → tap ＋ Money on Rashad.

You buy a fridge for 300 with his money → tap 🛒 Item on Rashad, write “fridge 300”.

The app now shows: Rashad leftover = 200. 🎉
You never have to remember — the app remembers for you.

💡 Good to know

•
It works offline — no internet needed after it opens.
•
Your numbers are saved only on your phone — private.
•
You can switch LBP / USD at the top.
•
Made a mistake? Tap the small × next to any entry to delete it.
